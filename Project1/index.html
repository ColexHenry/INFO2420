Web Site Critiques

Sablétine

Question 1

  • Answer: My first impression of the website was that I found the company's logo to be very visually striking and felt that it contrasted strongly with the rest of the elements such as the menu, paragraph text, and pink shop Sabletine advertisement. It's not necessarily a bad thing, but my first observation.

Question 2

  • Answer: Based on what is seen on the index.html page (homepage) and other pages, the scheme is to left align informational text and place a caption to the right for any images. As well as to place an image in the middle top of the website.

Question 3

  • Answer: I find the structure of the site to be a bit confusing. I find it confusing mainly for the points I provided above, but also because of how text is handled and not all text is aligned or left justified. Instead the creator opted to use a mix of the two styles.

Question 4

  • Answer: I find that the sites use of images and multimedia help to enhance the website and promote their services. I do find it a little bit odd that they do not have more images or a gallery where users can view the menu or popular picks for baked goods.

Question 5

  • Answer: The load time was not noticeable at all, the site loaded instantly with it's graphics. I presume this is mainly due to the fact it's not serving dynamic content? Unsure.

Question 6

  • Answer: Yes the text color and size is readable. The text alignment and font are different for parts of the website though and that may be odd. The background is a simple white with images showcasing their goods.

Question 7

  • Answer: The site did have a navigation bar in the form of a menu that is located in the upper middle of the webpage The navigation appears to be done fairly well with it's different options equal length from each other. It's naturally intuitive for most web users.

Question 8

  • Answer: Yes the site did have a color scheme and look/feel that was repeated on all pages. The style was a white background, menu in middle/top position, an image at the top, information on the left, and a caption for the image place underneath and to the right of the webpage.

Question 9

  • Answer: The website responds appropriately to smaller screens in that it scales exactly if you were on mobile or on desktop. Regardless of your platform you would get the same experience on the wesbite which is great.

Question 10

  • Answer: I found the alignment of text was oddly done, but otherwise the graphics and menu/navigation bar was done correctly. I believe that the hiring information and address information could probably have been located elsewhere instead of appearing on the homepage in the way it is displayed.
